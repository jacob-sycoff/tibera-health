Blueprint food routine (visual summary)
A quick visual based on Bryan Johnson’s “Protocol” page. This is not medical advice; it’s a readability layer on top of the source.
Early morning
Breakfast (pre‑workout)
  • Nutty pudding–style breakfast (protein + berries + fat)
  • Emphasis on repeatable routine
Late morning
“Super Veggie” meal
  • Legume base (e.g., lentils) + cruciferous veg
  • Flavor + acids + seeds + fermented foods
Noon
Final meal of the day
  • Example: stuffed sweet potato + beans + veg + healthy fats
  • Early cutoff (time‑restricted eating)
Source: Bryan Johnson’s Protocol. Details and quantities can change; always defer to the linked source and your clinician.
